Android virtual device emulators are known to be very slow. We can try few tweaks to make it faster. Faster CPU with more RAM will considerably increase the speed and responsiveness of the emulator. But low end computers are also can get a reasonable speed by tweaking few emulator settings.

ARM vs Intel x86

Intel x86 emulators are faster than ARM. Intel uses HAXM (Hardware Accelerated Execution Manager) to speedup the emulator performance. You can download HAXM in SDK manager. It is available in extras.
